The People

Building Nature's Vault

Our team is a collaboration between mining industry leaders, environmental experts, blockchain specialists, and creative thinkers, all driven by a common ambition to revolutionize the way the world thinks about mining and to achieve a net carbon neutral future.

Nature's Vault was founded by Phil Rickard, a global entrepreneur who has built successful enterprises across a range of industries, with a particular focus on mining. Against the backdrop of the climate crisis, and with an eye on the next generation, he realized a paradigm shift was needed in the way society invests in gold and the mining of it. And thus, he created Nature’s Vault.
