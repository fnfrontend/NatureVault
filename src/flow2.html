Introduction

Legacy Token

Legacy Tokens are digital tokens issued on the Binance Smart Chain as BEP20 tokens. Each Legacy Token is backed by NaturesGoldTM, the preservation of at least 1/100th of a gram of in-ground gold in deposits quantified by independent experts and controlled through Mining Rights owned by Nature’s Vault.

Real Gold Each Legacy Token is linked to real in-ground gold, quantified by either JORC or NI 43-101 technical reports.

Why Do We Need to Mine?

Gold in
An Open Mine

The global mining industry is responsible for 4-7% of global GHG emissions; the industrial activity needed to mine just one ounce of gold releases on average 800 kg of CO2

Every year, global mining companies dump more than 180 million tonnes of hazardous mine waste into rivers, lakes and oceans worldwide.

For gold mining, 99% of mined ore is discarded as waste.

Despite the enormous environmental cost, roughly 50% of gold mined annually is simply stored away in bank vaults.

Gold In
Nature’s Vault

NaturesGoldTM prevents the release of direct and indirect GHG emissions from the activities of the mining industry.

The preservation of NaturesGoldTM protects local biodiversity, waterways, and surrounding communities from the effects of mining.

Instead of waste, the positive impacts of in-ground preservation can be quantified by industry standard organizations in the form of carbon credits

NatureGoldTM is tradable through the Legacy Token and spares the planet of the environmental impacts of both real world and cryptocurrency Proof of Work mining.

What is Legacy Token?

Legacy Tokens are digital tokens issued on the Binance Smart Chain as BEP20 tokens. Legacy Token represents a trailblazing approach forinvesting in Natural Capital – the planet’s natural resources or assets and the life-sustaining ecosystem benefits they provide. Moreover,Legacy Tokens represent a critically needed net-zero bridge between a dominant real world store of value - Gold - and the crypto world.

Each Legacy Token is backed by NaturesGoldTM, the preservation of at least 1/100 of a gram of in-ground gold in deposits quantified byindependent experts and controlled through Mining Rights owned by Nature’s Vault. We plan to progressively add more Mining Rights and therefore gold deposits to the inventory of gold preserved by each Legacy Token.
